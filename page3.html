Apple Butter Festival

The Historical Society of Grand Rapids sponsors the annual Applebutter Fest as an event that brings people together, both from the local community and cities far and near. There are no employees; an allvolunteer force works throughout the year to coordinate this event that often hosts over 40,000 visitors.
